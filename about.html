About

I am a follower of Christ and a servant of the Lord. I created these tools to serve His Kingdom. Faith-based and Spirit-guided.

I started writing code in my teens, inspired by The Social Network (2010). But the story goes back further than that.

My first interaction with a computer was met with punishment. I could barely speak and I was already clicking around on a computer. I printed a page in Web Dings because I was randomly clicking around and accidentally hit print.

I started playing games on the computer. Then online games. And then I learned almost everything I needed to know from a computer. Computation became fascinating when I studied the mathematics in algorithmic science: flow, dynamic programming.

Now, I create software, ordained by the Lord, a humble servant. My goal is to spread His Gospel. And also serving as a computer-wiz, in all aspects of computation including hardware and digital consulting, and focusing specialized services on custom digital developments such as software, mobile applications, custom test applications, desktop applications, enterprise applications, and many more.
