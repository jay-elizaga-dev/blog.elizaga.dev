Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

This site respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data Collection

This site is a static website. It does not use cookies for tracking. It does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through a consultation booking or donation).

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Sanity CMS — Content management and delivery. Sanity's privacy policy applies to data stored in their platform.
  • Buy Me a Coffee — Donation processing. Their privacy policy applies when you make a donation.
  • consultation.elizaga.dev — Consultation booking. Any data you provide during booking is handled according to the booking platform's privacy policy.

Analytics

This site does not currently use analytics tracking.

Local Storage

This site stores your theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out via the consultation page.
